About Us

Employees are the backbone of any company therefore their management plays a major role in an organization. Monitask makes it easy for the administrator to keep track of all the records of employees and allows to manage all the activities related to employees. Moreover, the admin can assign tasks to employees and assess their progress in order to keep track of employee’s performance. HR manager manages employee’s attendance and their payroll. Employee’s attendance are being marked by using RFID technology. Payroll of all the employees is being managed on the basis of employee’s attendance and generate pay slips. An admin can even check employees belong to which department and working on which client’s project. This system provides the details of all the clients and current progress of the project. Employees can also check their attendance and payroll by using an app. An employee can also view and edit his profile.
